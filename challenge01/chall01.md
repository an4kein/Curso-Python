#### Create a script that read the day, month, and year of birth and shows a formatted message.
```

# by anakein
# Create a script that read the day, month, and year of birth and shows a formatted message.

day   = input('Day: ')
month = input('Month: ')
year  = input('Year: ')

print('You were born on', month, day, year)
```
